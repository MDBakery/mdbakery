About Us

Our History

MD Bakery's journey started in 2003 by two brothers as a strategy to introduce quality bakery products exclusively in their hometown. The immense popularity earned in the early period of its introduction laid the foundation for what was to be a truly remarkable story of success.

Our Mission

At MD Bakery, our mission is to provide freshly baked goods to end customers through our franchise model while giving business opportunities to franchisees without the need for baking knowledge. We offer a wide variety of bakery products and confectionery, ensuring that there is something for everyone to enjoy.

At MD Bakery, we are committed to quality, freshness, and customer satisfaction. We use only the finest ingredients and traditional baking techniques to ensure that every bite of our products is a delicious and memorable experience. Our franchisees receive extensive training and support to ensure that they provide the same level of quality and consistency to their customers.

Our bakery franchise model allows aspiring entrepreneurs to join our network and start their own bakery business with the support of our established brand, proven systems, and ongoing training and support. Our bakery B2B service allows businesses to order our freshly baked goods and confectionery for their customers or events.

Our Vision

Our vision at MD Bakery is to become the leading provider of freshly baked goods and confectionery in India. We strive to achieve this by continually innovating and improving our products, expanding our franchise network, and delivering exceptional customer service. We aim to be a trusted and respected brand that is synonymous with quality, freshness, and reliability. Ultimately, we want to share the joy of baking with people everywhere and make every bite a memorable experience.
